My research is developed within national and international research networks focused on comics studies, media ecology, and visual culture.

## Research networks

- **SnIF – Studying ’n’ Investigating Fumetti**  
  Italian research network dedicated to the history, theory, and sociology of comics.  
  https://grupposnif.it/

- **DiCuS Lab – Dipartimento di Scienze Umane, Filosofiche e della Formazione (University of Salerno)**  
  Research laboratory focused on cultural processes, media, and social transformations.  
  https://www.disps.unisa.it/dipartimento/strutture?id=740

- **COMICS Research Group (Ghent University)**  
  International research group dedicated to comics studies, visual narratives, and media history.  
  https://www.comics.ugent.be/

- **ACME – Applied Cultural Media Ecology (University of Liège)**  
  Research group working on media ecology, cultural analysis, and contemporary media environments.  
  https://acme.phl-lab.uliege.be/
