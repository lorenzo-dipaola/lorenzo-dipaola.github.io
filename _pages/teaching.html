--- title: "Teaching" permalink: /teaching/ --- My teaching activity focuses on media studies, comics studies, visual culture, and cultural sociology. I have taught and delivered seminars at undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral level, combining historical approaches with media theory and critical analysis of contemporary cultural forms. ## Courses taught - **Theories and Sociologies of Comics** MA programme – University of Salerno Course focusing on the history, theory, and sociology of comics, from early print cultures to digital comics. The course combines media ecology, cultural studies, and visual analysis. ## Guest lectures and invited seminars - **Between Ruins and Possible Worlds: Post-Apocalyptic Narratives in Comics and TV Series** University of Naples Federico II (2024) - **For a Fistful of Celebrity: Sport, Media, and Stardom in Italian Culture** Doctoral Seminar – University of Salerno (2024) - **From Print to Digital: Histories and Paths of an Inafferrable Medium** University of L’Aquila – Centre for Transcodification Studies (2022) - **The Image from Hypertext to Transmediality** Master’s Programme in Digital Communication – University of Messina (2021) - **Virtual Experiences: Bodies and Society between VR and AR** University of Salerno (2021) ## Academic supervision and activities - Seminar organisation and moderation within the MA programme *Theories and Sociologies of Comics* University of Salerno - Teaching activities connected to national and international research projects My teaching approach emphasizes close reading of media objects, comparative analysis, and the integration of historical sources with contemporary media theory.
